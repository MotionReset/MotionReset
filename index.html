How Does Sports Massage Help?

Sports massage is designed to enhance athletic performance and recovery. Here are some key benefits:

  • Reduces muscle tension and pain
  • Improves circulation and flexibility
  • Enhances recovery by reducing muscle fatigue
  • Prevents injuries by maintaining muscle health
  • Supports mental relaxation and reduces stress
